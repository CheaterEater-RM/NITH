# Not in the Hospital! (NITH)

Drop pod raiders can only land under open sky. Constructed roofs and thin rock roofs block all hostile drop pod arrivals — raiders must land outside and walk in.

## What it does

In vanilla RimWorld, drop pod raiders can punch through any non-mountain roof to land exactly where your pawns are clustered — inside your hospital, nursery, or bedrooms. NITH removes this. Raiders targeting your colony will now land in the nearest open-sky area large enough to hold them and walk from there.

If your base has no open-sky area large enough to hold the raid, raiders arrive as a standard walk-in instead.

## What it doesn't change

- Friendly drops (allied faction aid, royal permits)
- Trade drops
- Your own drop pods
- Shuttle landings
- Mech cluster building placement
